Alabama Court System Overview

The Alabama court system includes several levels of courts, each with specific jurisdiction and roles.

  • Municipal Courts – Handle city ordinance violations and misdemeanors.
  • District Courts – Hear preliminary felony matters, misdemeanors, and civil cases under certain amounts.
  • Circuit Courts – General jurisdiction over felony criminal cases and major civil disputes.
  • Alabama Supreme Court – The highest appellate court in the state.
