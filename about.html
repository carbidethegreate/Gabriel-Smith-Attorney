About Gabriel Smith

Gabriel Smith is a lifelong Alabama resident and dedicated attorney with over a decade of experience. He graduated cum laude from the University of Alabama School of Law and began his career clerking for the Honorable Patricia Sellers.

In 2013, he established his own practice in Opelika, where he combines small-town values with big-firm expertise to deliver personalized, effective representation for every client.

  • Bar Admission: Alabama (2012)
  • Member: Alabama State Bar – Criminal Justice Section
  • Recognition: 2024 Super Lawyers “Rising Star”

Gabriel is committed to clear communication, thorough preparation, and achieving the best possible outcomes. Whether you need help with criminal defense, personal injury, or another legal matter, he will stand by your side every step of the way.
